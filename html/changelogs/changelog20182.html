  • Ravager reworked, gains two new abilities (ravage and second wind) and one reworked ability (eviscerating charge), as well as the Rage mechanic. Ravage allows to attack up to 3 adjacent foes and Second Wind spends all the rage to heal and regenerate plasma.

Surrealistik updated:

While on Harm intent, Crest Fling will throw the target in front of the Crusher, otherwise, it will throw the target behind the Crusher. Deals minor Brute and moderate Halloss damage to non-Xenos that scales with the distance the target was flung. Halloss damage ignores armour.

Each unit of Halloss damage causes 1.5 units of pain that cannot be reduced by pain killers. If total pain exceeds 200, then a human will be instantly disabled for a significant duration; this is the primary mechanism by which marines can be captured.
