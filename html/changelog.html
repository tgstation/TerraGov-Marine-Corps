ma44 updated:

  • Xenomorphs will no longer attempt to slash destroyed (delimbed) limbs
  • Delimbs now occur based on thresholds instead of rng, see below for details
  • Delimbs occur when the limb has 75 or 100 brute damage occurred to it if it's a hand/foot or arm/leg respectively
  • When a limb is delimbed, only 25% of it's damage is transferred to it's "parent" (hand to arm to chest for example)
  • Bone breaks now only occur at 37 and 50 damage for hands/feet and arms/legs respectively
  • FOR XENOS: This means if you want to kill a marine as fast as possible, you target their chest, otherwise you can target a limb to disable a limb but expect a longer fight due to a decreased damage transfer upon limb dismembering
