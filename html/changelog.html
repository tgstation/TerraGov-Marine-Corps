July 26 2018

RavingManiac updated:

  • Barricades are no longer magical, and can block bullets both ways. The closer the shooter is to the barricade, the more likely his shot will pass. Shot accuracy improves the chances of passing the barricade. Clicking on the barricade itself will always hit it.
