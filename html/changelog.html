Surrealaser updated:

  • Hive Status now displays the current and maximum hit points and exact coordinates of each hive member, as well as max number of T2s and T3s the hive can support.
  • The Stagger debuff now halves all projectile damage.
  • Chairs, stools and most non-nest beds no longer block xeno buildables.
  • Integrity of most non-marine buildables/barricades significantly reduced.
  • Shields now reduce incoming damage by hard armor before applying soft armor reduction.
  • Shield cover ratings are now modified by statuses; KO/Sleep/Paralysis disables shield benefits entirely as they should. Stun/Knockdown reduces cover by 50%. Stagger reduces cover by 25%.
  • Shields regain their 5 hard armor vs melee in light of these changes.
  • Fixed Shields sticking to the back due to NODROP. You can no longer equip NODROP items to non-hand locations.
  • Barricades can no longer be anchored on the dropship or other no construction turfs.
  • Quickcades and Barbed Wire can no longer be built on the dropship and other no construction turfs.
  • Razorfoam will not produce barbed wire on no construction turfs.
