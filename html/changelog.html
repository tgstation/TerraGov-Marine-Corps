Rohesie updated:

  • Stamina type of damage added. It comes and goes faster than other damage types, knocksdown the victim if it has too much of it, and is otherwise harmless.
  • Walk mode made severely faster, and the default for humans. They can run by making use of a stamina buffer, which they recover when not running. Synths don't have stamina, so they cannot sprint/run but are unaffected by stamina damage.
  • Xenos now have melee armor. Humans also have melee accuracy, which is perfect while walking and only 80% when running. Their tackles now fullyrespect melee armor.
  • Neurotoxin no longer injects chems. It now deals direct stamina-damage, same as xeno tackles, resin spit and a few other things.
  • Examine is now a keybind, defaulted to Shift. It merges old shift+click and alt+click on a turf near you. Alt defaults to run-while-holding, and you can shoot or attack while doing so. You can rebind it as well.
  • The hunter is now much faster while stealthed, so as a consequence its sneak attack has been nerfed some. Probably needs further tweaks.
  • Marines are allowed to respawn after a certain time dead. We'll see how this goes.
  • Marines can no longer join with the same name of someone else who joined the game and didn't cryo.
  • Shuttles can no longer be called, redirected or summoned while in transit.
  • Removed the broken dropship shutter button. Marines can still lockdown the doors through the computer, but xeno rulers can unlock them.
  • Xenos that miss the hijack ride will be gibed, cleaning the planet, but increasing the number of stored larvas.
