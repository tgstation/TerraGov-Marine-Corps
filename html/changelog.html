Surrealistik updated:

  • Flare grenades are flares that can be fired from grenade launchers, and largely replace existing flares. They do minor burn damage on impact and inflict a small number of burn stacks if they impact someone while activated.
  • Impact nades are a new standard issue grenade that comes standard with basic kits (subject to change). Upon impact with anything while primed, they detonate in a powerful explosion, flashing adjacent squares.
  • UGL and grenade launcher grenade movement speed slightly increased.

  • Queen loses the gib ability, as this was a no brainer, unfun way to remove people permanently from a round.
  • Armors tweaked to account for energy/bio damage types closer to intention.
  • Glove acid resistance updated and slightly improved.
  • Larval growth sting added to drones.
  • Hunter sneak attacks can no longer trigger off pounce, but are more damaging and stagger + slow
  • Fixed sentries not spawning with ammo.

  • Supply drop pads fixed to work with lasgun power pack rechargers.
  • Misc Ravager rage fixes.

  • Assault shotgun uses specialist skill.
  • Movement during pounces causing wonky/unintended behaviour.
  • Minor sentry alert tweaks.
